About

A little bit about us!

  • May 2022

    Seeing An Opportunity

    Back in 2021, I worked as a real estate agent for a local brokerage in Utah County. Although it was a great job, I knew I wanted to transition into something different while still staying within the real estate industry.

  • July 2022

    Henline Co-Host Was Born

    That's when I was introduced to the idea of Airbnb "Co-hosting." After finding a mentor, I soon after created my own short-term rental management company called "Henline Co-Host."

  • November 2022

    Transition to Ha'le Rentals

    After spending some time in the management game, I knew the company needed a re-branding to put emphasis on helping guests feel at "home" during their stay. Thus, "Ha'le Rentals" was born. Ha'le is hawaiian for home.

  • Current

    Where We Are Now

    That brings us to now. Though still small, Ha'le Rentals manages a 2M+ portfolio and is growing with each new month. We hope you give us a call and decide to work with us!
